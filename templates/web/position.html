Royal Drive

Founder, Chairman & Managing Director

Royal Drive is the next level sales company of luxury pre-owned cars based in Kerala. Buying, selling & exchanging luxury cars is made easy by the company; the families also get a chance to take part in the purchase of pre-owned cars. Royal Drive introduced a luxurious garage for the sales of pre-owned luxury cars as real-time touch and feel experiences are necessary for pre-owned cars.

The business was inspired by European sales strategies of building amazing showrooms for pre-owned luxury cars. The increase in online sales of new cars and the necessity of broad showrooms for pre-owned cars paved the smooth passage to his business triumph.


RD Smart

Founder, Chairman & Managing Director

Royal Drive Smart is yet another business founded by Mujeeb Rahman. Continuing his glorious triumph over Royal Drive, the business expanded to the sales of normal cars of the price range of 5lakh to 15 Lakh. Moreover, he aims to open a platform for the common man to purchase normal cars as well as build strong relationships with prominent high-class figures.
